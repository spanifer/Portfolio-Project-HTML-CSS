Ford Model A - Tudor Sedan

The Sedan was probably the most popular of the body styles especially the 2 door. It was basically the family saloon of the Model A bodies and at $495 for the 2 door and $570 for the Fordor, it was great value and extreme luxury after the faithful Model T.

Ford Model A - Special Coupe

The difference between the Model A and Model T was like night and day. The engine was a water-cooled L-head inline four with a displacement of 201 cu in. This engine provided 40 hp (30 kW; 41 PS). Top speed was around 65 mph (105 km/h).

Ford Model T - Touring

We're looking at a 1919 Ford Model T Touring Car in black paint. A step back in time with a car that was affordable, and the first car mass produced on a moving assembly lines with completely interchangeable parts.
